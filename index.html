Reflection

When light hits a mirror, it reflects out at the same angle that it hit the mirror at. Try moving the light ray to see this -->

Law of Reflection
The angle with which light hits the mirror (θ i) is the same as the angle it reflects out at (θ r).

θ i = θ r
